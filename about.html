About Us

Welcome to Bloom Florists, your neighborhood floral haven! Nestled in the heart of the charming town of Willow Creek, we’ve been adding a splash of color and a touch of magic to our community since our doors first opened in the spring of 2010.

At Bloom Florists, we believe that every bouquet tells a story, and we’re here to help you share yours. Whether it’s a cheerful bunch of daisies to brighten a friend’s day or an elegant arrangement of roses to celebrate a special moment, we pour our hearts into every petal we touch.

Founded by the flower-loving duo, Emma and Laura, Bloom Florists started as a small dream to spread joy through the simple beauty of nature. Over the years, we’ve blossomed into a beloved local treasure, known for our unique arrangements, personalized service, and a shop that feels like a garden in full bloom—no matter the season.

Pop in to say hello and let us help you find the perfect bouquet for any occasion. At Bloom Florists, we’re more than just a flower shop—we’re a little corner of happiness, where every visit feels like a stroll through a meadow of dreams.
